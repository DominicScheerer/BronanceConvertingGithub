DESCRIPTION OF PROJECT
In our efforts to create a seamless experience for our customers, we have developed a platform that allows users to purchase sets of meals that are delivered directly to their homes. Our platform makes it easy for users to find practical options based on a variety of meal characteristics. Whether you are following a vegan, keto, or paleo diet, weâ€™ve got you covered! We also offer services for anyone without dietary restrictions; by using our service you can receive your macronutrient breakdown as well as what foods you need in your diet to stay healthy!

As a Bronance user, you are encouraged to make healthier choices!


HOW TO USE OUR WEBSITE We provide services that deliver food straight to your door! On this site you have the option to pick the best looking meals of your liking. Visit our Health Guidelines page for more information on what composition makes up a healthy diet. After choosing meals among our healthy choices, you may find yourselves at our Meal Offerings page. Here you can actually add the meals you would like to your cart. Lastly, if you navigate to our profile page you will either be directed to login or you will see your profile information. Here you can view your personal information you signed up with along with the ability to view your transaction history. In the future, this will give users the ability to track their orders as well as many other features!









Returning Customer?

We've got you covered. With your permission, we save your address, card information and even food choices to better determine the food choices that fit your dietary needs! In the future we plan to implement features that will give specific recommendations based on both, what is best for your health, and your preferences based on your order history'
